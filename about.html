About Us

Volunteer Eireann is a voluntary organisation that regularly holds nationwide events to encourage more people to become involved in volunteering around Ireland. Our aim is to promote, support and encourage the practice of volunteering. As with all charitable organisations, we rely heavily on fundraising. Fundraising events are held at both a national and local level.

By browsing through our website, you can learn about the range of services we provide and all aspects of our work. If you are interested in more detailed or specific information about the work we do or any of our events, make sure to contact our team. Alternatively, you can sign up to receive one of our information packs.
